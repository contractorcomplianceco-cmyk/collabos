9. MIND MELD ROOM

Build the Mind Meld Room to match the attached Mind Meld mockup as closely as possible.

This is a private Rose/Carmen collaboration space.

Purpose:
Rose and Carmen can review each other’s work, see Rose OS feedback, add thoughts, and hand work back and forth.

Core workflow:
- Rose can review Carmen’s work, systems plans, build notes, CRM/automation ideas, process structures, and implementation reports.
- Carmen can review Rose’s ideas, company direction, product concepts, client-facing thoughts, strategy notes, and build requests.
- Rose OS generates a private synthesis for each item.
- Rose can press “Ready to Carmenfy.”
- Carmen can press “Ready to Rosify.”

Button behavior:
- “Ready to Carmenfy” routes the item to Carmen for systems, process, CRM, automation, architecture, or build-readiness review.
- “Ready to Rosify” routes the item to Rose for founder vision, company direction, public positioning, client-facing implications, or final decision review.
- These actions update local state and create a private handoff item.
- They do not create official company decisions.

Mind Meld views:
- Rose View
- Carmen View
- Shared Mind Board
- Report Exchange
- Thought Layers
- Handoff History
- Private Decision Notes

Each Mind Meld item includes:
- title
- source
- owner
- current status
- Rose thoughts
- Carmen thoughts
- Rose OS synthesis
- open questions
- alignment status
- risk level
- privacy level
- next handoff
- final outcome
- audit/history timeline

Innovative Mind Meld functions:

1. Alignment Meter
Shows where Rose vision and Carmen systems/readiness are aligned, partly aligned, or missing context.

2. Translation Lens
Converts Rose’s strategic idea into Carmen-ready process/build requirements.
Converts Carmen’s system notes into Rose-ready business implications.

3. Decision Heatmap
Highlights which items need Rose decision, Carmen systems review, both, or no decision yet.

4. Friction Detector
Identifies where an idea is exciting but operationally unclear, or where a system is strong but needs founder voice.

5. Question Composer
Drafts the 3-5 highest-value questions Rose should ask Carmen or Carmen should ask Rose.

6. Merge Magic
Identifies two separate Rose/Carmen ideas that could become one stronger structure.

7. Readiness Passport
Checklist for:
- vision readiness
- systems readiness
- data readiness
- privacy readiness
- build readiness
- launch readiness

8. Time-Saver Brief
Creates a one-page “what you need to know before responding” summary.

9. Decision Echo
Captures what changed after Rose/Carmen feedback so the reasoning can be preserved.

10. Sensitive Flag
Marks private leadership content that should not enter general Company Brain records without explicit approval.

Mind Meld guardrails:
- Private to Rose and Carmen unless explicitly shared.
- Rose/Carmen notes do not automatically become company decisions.
- Rose OS synthesis remains a recommendation.
- Sensitive, private, HR, financial, legal, client, and leadership-only content must stay permission-protected.
- Items can become proposed company-record updates only after Rose or Carmen sends them to review.
